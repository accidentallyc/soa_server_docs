Patch 2.0
Dont Starve Mine Together

  • The following actions now induce Exhaustion once you perform it a certain number of times in a certain time frame (3 minutes).
    • Hard Working
      • Used pickaxe, axe, sword, axe and fishing rod 200 times
    • Traveling Fatigue
      • Travelled a total of 500 blocks using boat, horse, and pig
  • Exhaustion
    • Effects
    • Enduces hunger
    • Slow I
    • Mining Fatigue I
    • Slightly reduces insanity
  • Insanity System
    • There will be an internal counter for Sanity that starts at 0.
    • Causes of Insanity
      • Exhaustion adds -1
      • Dying resets your sanity to -10
    • Curing Insanity
      • Curing
      • It disappears after 30 seconds
      • Can be cured by drinking water or milk
      • Lying down on a bed
    • Effects of Insanity
      • Level 1 (-11)
        • You hear random sounds
      • Level 2 (-21)
        • You hear more sounds
        • You get damaged randomly by a non existent zombie
        • People message you even if they really didn't
      • Level 3 (-31)
        • You get damaged by non-existent phantoms
  • Custom Player Dungeons
    • There are currently 2 active dungeons (Great Pharaos Tomb and Shang Tsung's Tomb) around the world that you can attempt to find
    • These dungeons will have some varying loot and will always have atleast 1 unholy weapon in the end
    • We suggest you only attempt to subjugate it once you are fully armored as you are likely to die inside
